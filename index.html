About

Graduating with a Bachelor's degree of Science in Packaging Engineering from Michigan State University in 2016, I have always enjoyed the challenge of solving complex problems. Seeking further exposure, curiosity led me astray, as I began to pursue a multitude of different professional and entreprenuerial ventures across other industries. Throughout these experiences I began to develop a newfound appreciation for the sheer magnitude of software and technology, having relied on them so heavily in my various endeavors. This new perspective inspired me to begin experimenting with code back in 2019. While I enjoyed the curriculum, understanding just how big of a commitment it was, I wasn't at the time, in a position to go all in. Fortunately the stars aligned for me in early 2022, and eager to reconnect with my engineering side, I decided to take the leap of faith and enroll in a Full-Stack Software Engineering bootcamp at Flatiron School. With a much refined skill-set, I graduated in July of 2022, and haven't looked back. I am extremely excited to parlay not only these technical engineering skills, but also my diversified background, and creativity, to contribute to the next wave of empowering technology.
